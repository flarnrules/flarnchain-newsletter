What even is a blockchain?

This is a chain of blocks

This is the way I envision the chain of blocks. Like a growing tower up into the sky, continuously rising into the cosmos. Each new block points to the previous block. This creates an unbreakable chain. You can't change information in any new blocks without altering the information in all of the blocks before it, making it really nice for recording transactions. like a ledger.
